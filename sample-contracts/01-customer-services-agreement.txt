CUSTOMER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Customer Services Agreement is entered into between Northstar Retail Inc.
("Customer") and DemoTech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Provider").

Effective Date: June 1, 2026
Expiration Date: May 31, 2027
Contract Value: USD 420,000

1. SERVICES
Provider will operate the inventory analytics platform used by Customer.

2. SERVICE LEVEL
Provider guarantees 99.9% monthly service availability. If availability falls
below 99.9%, Customer may claim service credits equal to 10% of the monthly fee.

3. SECURITY INCIDENTS
Provider must notify Customer of any confirmed security incident affecting
Customer Data within 24 hours after discovery.

4. DATA RETENTION
Provider must retain Customer Data for seven years after creation unless
Customer instructs Provider to delete it earlier.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Customer owns all reports, configurations, integrations, and deliverables
created specifically for Customer under this Agreement.

6. LIABILITY
Provider's liability for confidentiality, data-security, and intellectual
property claims is unlimited. Liability for all other claims is capped at
USD 1,000,000.

7. RENEWAL
This Agreement automatically renews for an additional one-year term unless
either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days before
the Expiration Date.

8. TERMINATION
Customer may terminate for material breach if Provider does not cure the breach
within 15 days after receiving written notice.

This sample agreement is fictional and is provided only for product testing.
