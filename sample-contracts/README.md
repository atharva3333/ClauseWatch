# Sample Contract Portfolio

These fictional contracts are designed to test ClauseWatch.

Upload them in this order:

1. `01-customer-services-agreement.txt`
2. `02-cloud-vendor-agreement.txt`
3. `03-platform-agreement-2025.txt`
4. `04-platform-renewal-2026.txt`

Expected portfolio risks include:

- Customer SLA of 99.9% versus vendor SLA of 99.5%
- Customer breach notice of 24 hours versus vendor notice of 72 hours
- Seven-year customer retention obligation versus 30-day vendor deletion
- Customer unlimited security liability versus tightly capped vendor liability
- Liability cap reduced from USD 500,000 to USD 50,000
- Renewal notice period increased from 30 to 60 days
- Intellectual-property ownership narrowed in the renewal

The agreements are synthetic and are not legal templates or legal advice.
