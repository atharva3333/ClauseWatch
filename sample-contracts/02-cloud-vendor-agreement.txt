CLOUD VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Cloud Vendor Services Agreement is entered into between DemoTech Solutions
Pvt. Ltd. ("Customer") and Nimbus Hosting Ltd. ("Vendor").

Effective Date: June 1, 2026
Expiration Date: May 31, 2027
Contract Value: USD 96,000

1. HOSTING SERVICES
Vendor will provide the cloud infrastructure used to operate Customer's
inventory analytics platform.

2. SERVICE LEVEL
Vendor targets 99.5% monthly service availability. Service credits are
available only if availability falls below 99.0%. Service credits are
Customer's sole remedy for an availability failure.

3. SECURITY INCIDENTS
Vendor will notify Customer of a confirmed security incident without undue
delay and no later than 72 hours after confirmation.

4. DATA RETENTION
Vendor may delete hosted data 30 days after termination of this Agreement.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Vendor retains ownership of its platform, APIs, templates, and any derivative
works created through use of Vendor's proprietary deployment tools.

6. LIABILITY
Vendor's aggregate liability arising from this Agreement will not exceed the
fees paid by Customer during the three months preceding the claim. Vendor will
not be liable for lost profits, lost data, or indirect damages.

7. RENEWAL
This Agreement automatically renews for one year unless Customer provides
written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days before the Expiration Date.

8. TERMINATION
Customer may terminate for material breach only if Vendor does not cure the
breach within 30 days after receiving written notice.

This sample agreement is fictional and is provided only for product testing.
